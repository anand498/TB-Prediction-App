About

Smart Diagnose is a web app that uses Computer Vision to predict weather you have pneumonia or not from the image of your lung x-ray. The deep learning model was trained on mobilenet with pretrained weights of imagenet(though all layers were trainable), GlobalAveragePooling(GAP) was used to flatten the CNN layer and prediction were made from GAP layer with use of any dense layer.
